Research into Text-to-SQL

Database and Data Mining Lab in POSTECH
Feb. 2018 to Dec. 2019

[Publication] Kim, H., So, B., Han, W., and Lee, H., "Natural language to SQL: Where are we today?," In 46th Int'l Conf. on Very Large Data Bases (VLDB) / Proc. the VLDB Endowment (PVLDB), Aug. 2020
This paper provides a survey and a taxonomy of existing text-to-SQL models. We perform extensive experiments to compare text-to-SQL models and show that accuracy measures used in previous studies are misleading.
As the second author of the paper, I perform most of the experiments and participate in developing the validation tool to measure the semantic accuracy of the models.
