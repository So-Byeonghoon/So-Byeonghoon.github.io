A survey on text-to-SQL parsing models

At
Database and Data Mining Lab in POSTECH
Apr. 2018 to Dec. 2019

[Publication] Kim, H., So, B., Han, W., and Lee, H., "Natural language to SQL: Where are we today?," In 46th Int'l Conf. on Very Large Data Bases (VLDB) / Proc. the VLDB Endowment (PVLDB), Aug. 2020

- This paper provides a survey and a taxonomy of existing text-to-SQL models. We performed extensive experiments to compare text-to-SQL models, showed that accuracy measures used in previous studies are misleading, and compared the accuracy of the models correctly.

- As the second author of the paper, I collected a data set, performed most of the experiments, and participated in developing the validation tool to measure the semantic accuracy of the models.
